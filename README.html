Title
Bike crime in Berlin (2021-2022)
Group members
David Guilllet and Laurens Duin (FT-JAN-2023-BER)
Goal of the project
The goal of this business intelligence (BI) project is to gain insights into bike crime in
Berlin over the period 2021-2022. For example, where are most bikes stolen, what
can we tell about the locations where they’re stolen from (any similarities) etc. Also
the time aspect could be interesting.
